About Maijah Taejon

Maijah Taejon is a singer-songwriter whose soulful R&B-pop vibes, fearless style, and magnetic energy are making waves in the music scene. Maijah is crafting a sound and persona that’s all her own—bold, authentic, and impossible to ignore.

Her music is a vibe—a multi-genre pop style. The kind you blast on a road trip or lose yourself in during a late-night playlist session. She is no stranger to stealing the show, and her performances of “The Star-Spangled Banner” at sporting events are cementing her as a rising star with a voice that commands attention.

Maijah is starting out with an impressive track record and showing no signs of slowing down. Her goal: to be the greatest singer-songwriter of her generation. With 28 tracks currently in development, including 5 Christmas songs, she’s building an arsenal of tunes.

Maijah is also open to collaborations, solidifying her ability to work with various artists. In fact, twelve beats produced by AporkALYPSE_NoW feature her vocals.

Upping her game, she is now working with her new engineer/Producer, Andy Freeman, and seeking the perfect microphone to match her signature sound.
